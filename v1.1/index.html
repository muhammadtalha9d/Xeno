FAQs about XeNo AI

How does XeNo keep my data private?

XeNo processes all files strictly on your local hardware. No data is ever uploaded to a central server or used for training cloud models.

What file types are supported?

XeNo supports PDFs, Microsoft Word (DOCX), Excel (XLSX), and all major image formats (JPG, PNG) for search and OCR extraction.

Is there a limit to the number of files I can index?

There is no software-imposed limit. XeNo is limited only by the available storage space on your device.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. All AI processing, searching, and organizing happens entirely offline on your local device.

Is there a mobile version available?

Yes. XeNo is available for iOS, Android, macOS, and Windows with local-first security.
